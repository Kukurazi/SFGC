Attack of Titans

Frames
Plot
Characters
Features
According to the manga tie, 100 years before its events began, humanity was almost completely destroyed by an unknown humanoid race of huge creatures called "titans." People were forced to take refuge behind the three high walls given the names Sina, Rosa and Maria. So people lived for many years thinking they were completely safe. Behind each wall on the outside were built "districts" - campuses serving as the outer border of defense and also surrounded by walls. "Titans" are carnivorous giants, resembling people, but with huge height - from three to fifteen meters, and in manga there appear creatures of even more monstrous size. The Titans wear no clothing and have various features, whether it 's a lack of skin, a huge torso or saw teeth. They devour people, though they do not need food; Move in both a comical and intimidating manner. Manga author Hadzime Isayama, answering the question why he chose giants as opposing monsters, answered: "Giants are disgusting, that is why." The image of destructive titans could be influenced not so much by the theme of huge kaiju monsters like Godzilla or Gidora, favorite by Japanese cinema, but by the paintings of Spanish artist Francisco Goya. Several military units appear in the manga designed to protect people 's settlements from the Titans. Soldiers are trained to fight and kill titans using a "spatial maneuvering device," abbreviated to UPM. It allows movement in three-dimensional space, clinging to objects and projections, and allows to reach the only weak point of titans - the base of the neck. Garrison soldiers guard the walls, but the only people who go beyond them are members of the intelligence unit. There is also a Royal Guard performing the role of police and guarding the King.
