Residents of a quiet American town witness the dazzling outbreak and fall of a huge facility. Days later, a boy named Hogarth Hughes finds a giant robot flying to Earth from a distant space galaxy in the vicinity. Despite its formidable look, the alien guest turns out to be the kindest creature. There is a real friendship between him and the brave boy. But the government and the military, who learned of the robot 's existence, decided to destroy the alien. Hogarth is the only one who can save the steel giant.
